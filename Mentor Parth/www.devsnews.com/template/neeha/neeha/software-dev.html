Web Development Process We Follow

Discover

It is the first phase where our developers gather all the requirements and goals from the clients.

Explore

Our team of experts explores the best solutions and strategies. We discuss the essential aspects of the design as well as the functionality of the end product.

Wireframe Design

Our team begins with the design of the wireframe. We keep improving it until the client is satisfied with the wireframe and shows us the green flag to move to the next phase of web development.

Build

Based on the approved wireframe, our professional web developers build the frontend and backend of the website.

Launch

Once the client is satisfied with the web design and functioning according to his goals, our team launches the website on the live servers.

Support

Mentor Parth Tech believes in scaling up the clients’ websites. Therefore we offer remarkable website support and maintenance services.
