Our Expertise

The relationship between brands and customers is known as branding. Brand identity is how a company conveys its attitude, essence, and tone to customers. Of course, emotions and memories customers carry for a long time with brands. In creating such a visual identity, graphic design is a vital aspect. We build professional graphic design for brands.

Visual Hierarchy :

Following a visual hierarchy is the best approach to increase the design value. It is all about the alignment of fonts, colors, and text sizes. Being in graphic design services, ITech-Mentor team can deliver the best outcome for clients.

Repetition :

The principle of repetition is related to branding. When logo colors are repeated in the design, it strengthens the overall design, and users can identify and recall the brands.

Balance :

Balance is an essential element in Graphic design. It offers stability and structure to the particular design. A blend of images, colors, and text can form complete design. There are two types of balance. Symmetrical and asymmetrical – Symmetrical is a yardstick to balance the design of both sides, and the asymmetrical balance is all about using colors and scale to handle the flow.

Logo :

It is a graphical representation of an organization or firm to convey what the company is all about. A well-designed logo can encourage businesses to get more users, whereas a poorly designed logo will drive away prospective customers. Therefore, logos are designed for a visual representation of an organization.
