What makes us different?

Staff Augmentation has become a popular business strategy in the IT sector. Businesses that value professional services at a reasonable cost have quickly embraced this delivery model. Outsourcing companies increased the scalability of their services in response to market demands. Through IT staff augmentation, you fill the gaps in your team by hiring IT technicians on a short or long term basis according to your project requirements.

Frequently Asked Question

IT staff augmentation is the process of hiring parallel talent on a project. A software development firm undertakes multiple projects and it is not possible to hire talent across every domain of the field at times some projects require talent to be hired for only a short period of time and this is where staff augmentation comes into picture. IT staff augmentation allows you to remotely hire talent necessary for the delivery of a project without having to deal with the hassle of dealing with hiring processes and retaining employees.

IT Staff Augmentation has gained its fame in a short period of time because of the cost and effort it saves and can be easily implemented with ongoing projects as well. By implementing IT staff augmentation you simply reap the benefits of not having to deal with the hiring process and not having to worry about retaining as well. It is like gaining the best talent but only for the duration of the project and the best part is there is no infrastructure cost involved in it.

IT staff augmentation starts with analyzing your requirements with regards to the project, then comes the search and review part where you analyze the competency of various service providers and try to summarize what's available in the market. Once you know what you want and what's available you can go ahead with onboarding. Now there will be a project manager assigned to you through whom you can keep a tap of the progress that has been delivered and to fulfill any shortfall on the requirements.
