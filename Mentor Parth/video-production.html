Video Production

ITech-Mentor is a creative video production agency that specializes in producing entertaining and engaging videos. Being a class apart from a video production company in India, we can assist you to create mesmerizing videos that can be used as part of the video marketing strategy campaign, social media marketing, and TV commercials. Of course, we can develop short corporate animation videos that help you explain your products and services to your valued customers.

You will work with our creative directors to bring your vision to life. Our expert team knows that refreshing content and spectacle in visuals are the two elements that serve as the core for delivering magnificent videos. Be it corporate video or animated video, our team can ensure you with our captivating videos for your brands.


Our Services

What makes us different?

The video production process can be divided into three stages:

  • Pre-Production
  • Production
  • Post-Production

For a sturdy building, you require a strong foundation. Similarly, an eye-grabbing video needs a unique concept and script that can engage and strike a chord with your audience. Our team will always come up with never heard ideas that can ensure a huge success. If an idea is conceptualized, then writing a script is a copywriter’s task. ITech-Mentor has adept copywriters who weave it aesthetically around the central idea.

Once the pre-production phase is nearing completion, the exigent phase of production starts. It is a crucial phase for quality video production. Our team is like soldiers who assail onto the battlefield and work rigorously to get the best output. Shooting the winning video is an art, and it is not everybody’s cup of tea.

Post-production is the final stage in video production, and the technical team will review the shots again and again to choose. The editors will come on board to edit videos with the latest technologies. Our editors can perfectly blend the video creatively. Then the music department will enter and add the score that gives sole and life to the video.


Our Expertise

We believe that creative and hard-hitting videos can leave a lasting impact on the audience. Besides having prolific video makers, cameramen, avid script writers, and editors, we have advanced equipment along with state-of-the-art editing and dubbing suites to deliver quality videos for commercial ads, corporate videos, and documentaries.
